Technology Revolutionizes Modern Learning
How digital tools are shaping the next generation of self-taught professionals
By Prajon Shrestha

Learning today has transformed drastically, thanks to the availability of free and structured resources online. From web development to AI, passionate learners can now access the same material as students in top universities.

Self-taught individuals are proving their abilities by building impressive portfolios and contributing to open-source projects. With discipline and curiosity, the possibilities are endless in this new digital era of education.
